# NRMA Agentic Financial Intelligence — Design Ideas

## Approach A — Corporate Precision
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets modern enterprise SaaS
**Core Principles:** Grid discipline, typographic hierarchy, data-forward, restrained colour
**Color Philosophy:** NRMA deep navy (#0033A0) as the dominant authority colour, pure white for breathing room, a single electric cyan (#00BFFF) accent for interactive elements only
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column hero, left-heavy text with right-side floating diagram; sections alternate left/right anchoring
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rule dividers, monospaced labels on diagrams, oversized section numbers
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals additional context; scroll-triggered fade-ins are subtle and purposeful
**Animation:** Staggered entrance on scroll; no bouncing or playful easing — strict ease-out-cubic only
**Typography System:** DM Serif Display for headlines, Inter for body; strict weight contrast between H1 (700) and body (400)
**Probability:** 0.08

## Approach B — Blueprint Architecture
**Design Movement:** Technical Blueprint / Engineering Drawing aesthetic
**Core Principles:** Precision, transparency, layered information, technical authority
**Color Philosophy:** White background with NRMA blue (#0033A0) as the primary ink colour; subtle grid lines in pale blue (#E8F0FE) evoke engineering drawings; gold (#F5A623) used sparingly for callouts
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width sections with a persistent left-margin annotation column; architecture diagrams are the hero content
**Signature Elements:** Dashed connector lines between components, corner bracket decorations on cards, dot-grid backgrounds on key sections
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hovering on architecture nodes reveals tooltips with technical detail; the diagram is the centrepiece
**Animation:** Elements draw in from left like a pen tracing a blueprint; lines animate on scroll
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk for headings (technical, geometric), Source Sans Pro for body; monospaced font for code/data labels
**Probability:** 0.07

## Approach C — Executive Command Centre
**Design Movement:** Premium Enterprise Dashboard / C-Suite Presentation
**Core Principles:** Authority, clarity, forward momentum, data confidence
**Color Philosophy:** Rich white (#FFFFFF) base, NRMA deep blue (#003087) for primary elements, a warm mid-blue (#0055D4) for secondary elements, and a subtle light-blue tint (#F0F5FF) for section backgrounds — no dark mode, pure executive daylight
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with a bold diagonal accent stripe; sections use a magazine-style asymmetric grid with wide gutters; statistics displayed in large-format number cards
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal section dividers in NRMA blue, large KPI/stat callouts, architecture diagram embedded as an interactive layer
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll with section snap points; hover states on stat cards reveal supporting context
**Animation:** Numbers count up on scroll entry; section headers slide in from left; diagram nodes pulse gently
**Typography System:** Playfair Display for hero headline (authoritative, premium), DM Sans for all other text; large tracking on section labels
**Probability:** 0.09

---

## Selected Approach: C — Executive Command Centre

This approach best serves the audience (CTO, technology leadership, executive stakeholders) and the content (a strategic opportunity, architecture, and financial intelligence capability). The diagonal accent stripe gives the NRMA blue brand a dynamic, forward-moving quality without departing from the white-and-blue theme. The large KPI callouts make the opportunity tangible at a glance.
